About KMG Bangalore

Welcome to Krav Maga Global Bangalore. We provide professional self-defence training across civilian, women, kids, military and law enforcement groups. Our instructors are certified and focused on realistic, practical training.

Krav Maga classes were first started in Goa, India by Elroy Vaz in 2011. Rhys took over the leadership mantle of KMG (India) after the passing of Late Mr. Elroy Vaz, Regional Director (India), in 2020.

From 2012 till date KMG’s Krav Maga has been taught in accordance with the curriculum and guidance of Master Eyal Yanilov.

Vision Statement

The Vision of Krav Maga Global India is to empower people in our country to be able to defend themselves thus ensuring their own safety.
