What is Krav Maga?

Krav Maga is a practical and effective self-defence system developed for civilians and professionals. It focuses on simple, easy-to-learn techniques, situational awareness, and effective responses to threats.

At Krav Maga Global, we believe every individual deserves the confidence that comes from knowing they can protect themselves and the people who matter to them. Our training is rooted in Krav Maga’s practical and instinctive approach, making it a powerful choice for anyone exploring modern combat disciplines—whether they come from backgrounds like MMA, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), Judo, Boxing, or are stepping into self-defence for the first time. Students value our sessions for their clarity, realism and strong focus on building capability under pressure. From civilians to law-enforcement personnel, people train with us to develop sharp decision-making, effective striking, essential grappling fundamentals and close-range responses grounded in real-world situations. If you're looking for a training environment that builds resilience, skill and confidence—not aggression—KMG Bangalore offers a purposeful, structured and impactful path forward.
